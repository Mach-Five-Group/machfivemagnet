Practice areas

Four disciplines, practiced deeply

We do a small number of things at a high standard. If your matter sits outside these areas, we will say so on the first call and point you somewhere good.

I

Business formation

Entity choice, founder agreements, and equity set up properly the first time, so the paperwork never becomes the story.

  • Delaware and California entities
  • Founder, equity, and vesting agreements
  • Conversions and reorganizations

II

Contracts

Drafting and negotiating the agreements your revenue depends on, with terms you can actually operate under.

  • Customer and vendor agreements
  • Licensing and IP assignments
  • Terms of service and policy reviews

III

Disputes

Steady hands when a deal breaks down. Most of the matters we take resolve without anyone seeing a courtroom.

  • Partnership and shareholder disputes
  • Commercial litigation
  • Mediation and structured settlement

IV

Employment

Hires, exits, and policies handled before they become exposure, with counsel that respects both the law and the people.

  • Offer letters and handbooks
  • Executive separations
  • Contractor classification

The firm

A deliberately small firm

Ten attorneys. No leverage model, no rotating cast of associates learning on your invoice. You work with the partner you hired, start to finish.

  • Flat fees for defined work: formations, contract packages, policy suites
  • Phase budgets for disputes, agreed in writing before work begins
  • Responses within one business day, usually much faster
  • Advice framed around the business outcome, not the billable hour

B2B Lead Gen

Magnet for B2B Lead Gen

Replace long forms with conversations that qualify, route, and convert. Collect the same information in half the time.

Long forms kill conversion. Short forms miss context.

You know the tradeoff. A long form gets you qualified leads but tanks your conversion rate. A short form gets volume but buries your team in unqualified noise. Magnet eliminates the tradeoff.

A conversational flow collects the same information as a 10-field form, but each question builds on the last. It feels fast because it’s relevant. The prospect doesn’t think “I’m filling out a form.” They think “this company is paying attention.”

How lead gen teams use Magnet

Landing page qualification that replaces the static form below your hero. Ask about their challenge, their timeline, and their budget. Branch based on answers. Route high-intent leads to sales instantly and nurture the rest.

Gated content delivery that feels like a conversation, not a toll booth. “What topic are you most interested in?” leads to a relevant resource. The email capture happens naturally in the flow, not as a gate.

Multi-step qualification built into the flow. A “50+ employees” answer means something different than “just me.” Every lead lands in your inbox with the full conversation attached. No follow-up email to figure out who they are.

A/B tests on the conversation itself. Run two variants per magnet, serve each visitor a consistent split, and declare the winner in one click.

Connects with your stack

Install through Google Tag Manager or a single script tag on any landing page. Every lead carries UTM parameters and Google Ads click IDs, so attribution survives the handoff, and leads export to CSV for any CRM. Native CRM integrations and webhooks are on the roadmap.

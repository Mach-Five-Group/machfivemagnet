What people build

Flows, the welcome hub, triggers, and the scheduler: the same parts, combined four different ways.

Book an estimate

A short flow asks what the job is, then opens the scheduler. The lead arrives with a preferred day and time already on it.

Qualify before the call

Choice and text steps collect the context you'd ask for anyway. The first call starts with answers, not introductions.

Support intake

The Support button on the hub runs its own flow. Multi-select steps sort the request before it reaches you.

Exit-intent save

A trigger opens the magnet as the visitor leaves. One question is easier to answer than a form is to finish.

Why it converts

Abandoned isn't invisible

Every session becomes a journey, whether it was completed, left mid-way, or abandoned at the first step. You see exactly where each visitor stopped and which question costs you leads.

A time, not a callback

Book opens a day/time picker inside the panel. The lead arrives with the visitor's preferred slot on it, not a note that says "we'll call you."

The winner goes live

A/B tests split visitors consistently and score each variant's conversion. Declare a winner and that variant becomes the live config in one click.

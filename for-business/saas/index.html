Magnet for B2B SaaS

Turn pricing page visitors into qualified demos. Replace your static form with a conversation that books meetings.

The problem with SaaS demo forms

Your visitor clicks “Book a Demo.” They see a form with 8 fields. Name, email, company, role, company size, use case, timeline, budget. They fill in three and leave. You never hear from them.

Magnet replaces that form with a quick conversation. Two or three questions, branched based on their answers, and they’re looking at a calendar. The whole thing takes 30 seconds.

How SaaS teams use Magnet

Demo booking flows that qualify while they convert. Ask about team size and use case up front. Route enterprise prospects to sales and self-serve users to a free trial.

Trial activation prompts that appear when a user goes idle. A conversational nudge is harder to ignore than a banner. Ask what they’re trying to do and point them to the right feature.

Pricing page qualification that matches visitors to the right plan. Instead of a comparison table they have to decode, walk them through three questions and recommend a plan.

Journeys that show the leak. Every session is tracked: completed, in progress, abandoned at step 2 of 4. You see exactly where prospects drop out of the demo flow.

Connects with your stack

Booking happens inside the panel: the visitor picks a day and time, and their preferred slot is captured on the lead. Every lead exports to CSV for whatever CRM you run, and carries UTM and Google Ads click IDs so campaign attribution survives the handoff. Native CRM integrations are on the roadmap.

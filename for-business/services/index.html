Professional Services

Magnet for Professional Services

Turn your contact page into an intake process. Qualify prospects and book consultations before you pick up the phone.

Your contact form doesn’t tell you anything useful

“Name, email, message.” That’s what most service firm contact pages collect. Then someone on your team has to email back, ask qualifying questions, go back and forth, and eventually schedule a call. That’s three days of friction for what could be a 30-second conversation.

How service firms use Magnet

Consultation booking flows that collect context before the meeting. What do they need help with? What’s their timeline? What’s their budget range? By the time you sit down, you already know what they want.

Service matching that routes inquiries to the right team. A law firm can separate contract review from disputes. An accounting firm can separate tax prep from advisory. The prospect gets a faster response and the right person.

Intake questionnaires that replace PDF forms and email chains. Everything is captured in one conversation, saved on the lead, and ready for the first meeting.

One panel for every channel. The magnet opens on a welcome hub (Book, Chat, Call, Email, Support), and you turn on only the channels your firm actually staffs.

Connects with your stack

Booking happens inside the panel: the prospect picks a preferred day and time, and it’s captured on the lead. Every lead exports to CSV for your CRM or practice management software. Direct CRM integrations are on the roadmap.

Capabilities

Four shops under one roof

Beam line, plate, pipe, and custom fab run as separate cells with shared detailing and QC. One PO, one point of contact, one delivery schedule.

Structural Beams

Wide flange, HSS, channel, and angle processed on a CNC beam line and finished to your detail drawings.

  • W6 to W44 wide flange, HSS to 20 in square
  • Coping, cambering, and drilling per detail
  • Shop primer, galvanizing coordination, or bare
  • Sequenced delivery matched to erection plan

Custom Fabrication

Weldments built from your drawings or developed with our detailers, up to 60 feet and 40 tons per piece.

  • Stairs, platforms, skids, hoppers, frames
  • AWS D1.1 procedures, CWI inspection in house
  • Fit-up and trial assembly before shipment
  • Carbon, stainless, and abrasion-resistant plate

Plate and Sheet

Cutting, forming, and rolling in one cell, so formed parts move straight to weld without a second truck ride.

  • CNC plasma to 2 in, oxy-fuel to 6 in
  • 10 ft x 40 ft burn table, nested from your files
  • Press brake forming to 500 tons
  • Plate rolling to 1.5 in thickness

Pipe and Tube

Rolled, mitered, and fit to spool drawings for rack, header, and structural applications.

  • Carbon and stainless, schedule 10 to XXH
  • Flanged, beveled, or grooved ends
  • Rolled sections to 24 in diameter
  • Hydro test documentation on request

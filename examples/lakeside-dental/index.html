Services

Care for the visit you actually need

Whether it has been six months or six years, we start where you are. No lectures, no upselling, just a clear picture and honest options.

Checkups & cleanings

Sixty relaxed minutes: a careful clean, photos you can actually see, and a plan with no surprises.

  • Digital X-rays only when needed
  • Gum health tracked visit to visit
  • Gentle option for sensitive teeth

Whitening

In-chair or take-home, matched to your enamel and your timeline rather than a one-size tray.

  • Shade mapping before we start
  • Sensitivity-aware formulas
  • Visible results in one to two weeks

Clear aligners

Straighter teeth without the brackets, planned and monitored by the dentist who knows your mouth.

  • 3D scan, no impression goop
  • Check-ins every six weeks
  • Retainers included at the end

About the practice

A small practice on purpose

Dr. Maren Holt opened Lakeside in 2016 with one idea: appointments should never feel like a queue. We are a team of six, we book longer visits than most practices, and we explain everything twice if you want. Blankets, headphones, and a view of the water from the hygiene rooms do the rest.
